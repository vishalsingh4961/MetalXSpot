The Problem

Legacy metal trading is broken, slow, and fragmented.

Billions in inventory sit idle while buyers chase quotes across email threads. The friction is structural — and it costs both sides margin every single day.

Trapped capital

Independent stockists sit on $100k+ of excess or secondary inventory with no digital distribution channel.

Friction-heavy RFQ cycles

Procurement teams burn 3–5 days emailing back-and-forth just to verify grades, tempers and tolerances.

Paperwork & verification risk

Checking Mill Test Certificates, credit and cross-border counterparty risk stays opaque and manual.

Lost margins

Buyers overpay from regional lock-in; suppliers miss high-intent regional orders they never see.

The Platform

A neutral, structured B2B metal marketplace.

Company Profile

Who we are.

MetalXSpot is a digital procurement and inquiry engine for the global base- and non-ferrous metals trade. We operate a neutral marketplace — owned by no mill and no producer — where verified industrial buyers and independent stockists transact with structure, speed and a full audit trail.

Mission

Modernize how the world trades metal — turning idle inventory into liquid capital through automated inquiry matching and stock discovery.

Vision

Become the trusted operating system for cross-border metal trade across the GCC, South Asia and Southeast Asia.

  • Structure fragmented RFQ flows into comparable, spec-precise inquiries.
  • Match buyers to regional stockists holding the exact material in stock.
  • Verify Mill Test Certificates and trade documents in one auditable vault.
  • Layer escrow, trade finance and logistics onto settled transactions.
